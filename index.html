Frequently asked questions

Use this section to handle the first questions editors, publishers, and procurement teams usually ask.

Does ManuscriptIQ replace editors or peer review?

No. ManuscriptIQ is a decision-support system. It helps editors review, summarize, and prioritize information faster, but final editorial decisions remain human decisions.

Can it run locally instead of sending manuscripts to third-party cloud tools?

Yes. ManuscriptIQ is designed around local-first and institution-controlled deployment options, which can reduce unnecessary manuscript exposure.

Can we use our own reviewer or AE database?

Yes. The recommendation engine is designed to work with local Excel or structured expert databases and can be adapted to additional data fields.

Can the review template be customized for a specific journal?

Yes. Review structure, prompts, scoring weights, and decision wording can be configured to match a journal’s editorial criteria and policy.
